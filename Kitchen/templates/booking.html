Corporate Retreats

Our restaurant is a cut above the rest when it comes to providing an exceptional dining experience with its one-of-a-kind concept, elegant design and stunning decor. We take pride in offering a range of spaces for corporate events, each designed to cater to the specific needs of our guests. Our venue is equipped with various areas that can accommodate different group sizes, ensuring that each event is intimate and memorable. Whether you are hosting a business dinner, a cocktail reception or a team-building event, we guarantee that our team will make it an unforgettable experience.

As part of our commitment to providing a truly unique African dining experience, we offer two exquisite menus that are perfect for corporate or group events. Our "Kenyan Culinary Adventure" menu takes diners on a gastronomical journey through the rich flavors of Kenya's diverse regions. Alternatively, our "African Tasting Tour" menu allows guests to savor a variety of delicacies from across the continent, expertly crafted by our talented chefs. All menus are available on our Menu page section.
